    • Chandler Bing

    An executive in statistical analysis and data reconfiguration for a large, multinational corporation. Chandler hates this job, although it pays well. He attempts to quit during season one but is lured back with a new office and a pay raise.

    Monica Geller

    The "mother hen" of the group and a chef,known for her perfectionist, bossy, competitive, and obsessive-compulsive nature.

    Ross Geller

    Monica's "geeky" older brother, a palaeontologist working at the Museum of Natural History, and later a tenured professor of palaeontology at New York University.

    Rachel Green

    A fashion enthusiast and Monica Geller's best friend from childhood. Rachel first moves in with Monica in season one after nearly marrying Barry Farber.

    Joey Tribbiani

    A struggling actor and food lover who becomes famous for his role on soap opera Days of Our Lives as Dr. Drake Ramoray.

    Phoebe Buffay

    A masseuse and self-taught musician. As a child, Phoebe lived in upstate New York with her mother, until her mother committed suicide and Phoebe took to the streets.
